CSS Border Types Demonstration

Solid Border: A clear, continuous line around the element.
Dotted Border: A border made up of small, round dots.
Dashed Border: A broken border consisting of short dashes.
Double Border: Two solid lines creating a bold frame.
Groove Border: A carved-in look that gives a 3D effect.
Ridge Border: A raised border that appears elevated.
Inset Border: Creates a sunken, embedded appearance.
Outset Border: Makes the element look like it's popping out.
Multi-Colored Border: Each side has its own distinct color.
Rounded Border: Smooth, curved corners for a softer look.
